About

Find essential home, garden, and building material supplies at Kerala Stonex! Kerala Stonex is a newly opened building materials supplier store in Kallambalam, Azhamkonam. At Kerala Stonex, we undertake interlock work and sell cement bricks, red bricks, Msand, metal, plastering sand, gardening pebbles, plastic/ cement pots of various sizes, household items, and fruit plant saplings (Coconut, Guava, Mangosteen, Rambutan, Chikoo, Star Fruit, and much more!)

We're a local family-owned business hoping to make our construction materials supply readily available for guaranteed satisfaction! Our supplies are sold in both small and large quantities, you decide! Get these supplies in bulk at any time.

We have over 100+ varieties of interlock designs available and we will also provide the laying work for you.
